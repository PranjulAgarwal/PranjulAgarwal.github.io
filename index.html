F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions, allows you to find answers to all their questions in one place. Not only does it make life easier for you, it can save your company's time and resources.

  • Can you help me update my existing website that another web firm built?

    Yes, I can. My services includes updating, modifying and maintaining pre-built websites.

  • How long does it take to build a website?

    Our standard websites take approximately 9-120 days to create. Our E-commerce (online store) websites take approximately 160 days to create. This time will vary from project to project.

  • Can you help me write content for my website?

    Yes. It includes copy writing and editing in all of our proposals. I also build your sitemap and help structure the foundation of your website in the planning stages.

  • What are the platforms on which you develop apps?

    For now, I provide native mobile app development solutions on iOS and Android. I also offer hybrid/cross-platform mobile app development solutions on HTML5.

  • What are the stages involved in the mobile app development process?

    There are three stages in our mobile app development process: The first stage is of planning in which we, after understanding your business and your objectives behind developing a mobile app, will wireframe a solution. The second stage is the design and development stage wherein we will create designs, character models (2D or 3D), sound effects and will test and remove the bugs and errors in the app, if any. The third and final stage marks the launch of the app followed by the marketing of the app.

  • How many types of apps do you develop?

    I develop a wide variety of apps ranging from utilities, entertainment, travel, finance, social networking, productivity, weather, business, to gaming and children's apps.
